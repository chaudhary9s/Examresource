Delhi Police Constable Syllabus

1. General Knowledge/ Current Affairs

  • General Awareness

  • Geography
  • Economics
  • General Science(Physic , Chemistry, Biology)

  • Analogies
  • Similarities and Differences

  • Observation

3. Numerical Ability

  • Decimals
  • Fractions
  • L.C.M. & H.C.F.
  • Ratio & Proportion
  • Percentage
  • Average
  • Tables & Graphs
  • Time & Work
  • Time & Distance
  • Discount
  • Simple & Compound Interest
  • Area, Volume, and Surface Area Calculation

4. Computer Awareness

  • Basics of Hardware and Software
  • Windows Operating System
  • Internet Terms and Services
  • MS Office (Word, Excel, PowerPoint)
  • History of Computers
  • Networking and Communication
  • Database Basics
  • Basics of Hacking, Security Tools, and Viruses
  • Basic Internet Knowledge and Protocol
  • Memory and Storage Devices
  • Computer Shortcuts and Abbreviations

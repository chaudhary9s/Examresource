SSC CGL Tier 2 Exam Pattern 2025

After qualifying the SSC CGL Tier 1 exam, candidates will appear for the SSC CGL Tier 2 exam. It consists of Paper 1 and Paper 2. Paper 1 is compulsory for all posts, whereas Paper 2 is for candidates applying for the post of Junior Statistical Officer (JSO) in the Ministry of Statistics and Programme Implementation.

Sl. No Papers Posts Time Allotted
1 Paper I Compulsory for all posts 2 Hours 30 Minutes
2 Paper II Junior Statistical Officer (JSO) 2 Hours
